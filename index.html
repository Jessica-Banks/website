INGENIOUS GINGER

Transportive and Seductive, Ingenious Ginger offers an instant sense of nature in a blissful and sultry destination. A solar perfume of sun-kissed mandarin, spicy ginger, elegant white florals, and warm, enveloping amber that cascades across your skin.

BUY NOW

An olfactory imagining of the unscented torch ginger lily flower, Ingenious Ginger is an abstract citrusy, floral, and ambery perfume that expresses our Founder's most beloved tropical flower, which grows in abundance at his holiday home in the wet tropics. This uplifting perfume opens with a burst of bergamot, amplified by the effervescent scent of ginger, the sparkle of mandarin, and the bright and sunny aura of magnolia.

To create an accord to represent the imagined beauty of the unscented torch ginger lily flower, Goldfield & Banks has used an olfactory native material - Red Back Ginger - as the starting point for this accord, rather than deriving it from an extract. Together with bergamot, mandarin, magnolia, vanilla, musk, and amber, perfumer Hamid Merati-Kashani realised Dimitri Weber's scented vision for this flower.

With Ingenious Ginger, Goldfield & Banks is the first perfume house to explore Australian Red Back Ginger as an olfactory material and to add this complex native botanical to its all-Australian luxury perfume collection.

OUR STORY

OUR PERFUMES

Combining the science of Australia's most authentic native essences with centuries of French expertise in the art of perfume making, our perfumes created in Australia are a quest for the ultimate Australian olfactory experience. Australian native botanical essences have been carefully selected to create a new and haunting Australian dream - one of burning deserts, sparkling coastal vistas and deep, beautifully scented forests.

With elements rarely used in modern perfumery, each has been carefully unravelled to expose its honest and stunning beauty. All our fragrances are perfumes (at least 20% scented essences), so enjoy the richness of their high concentration. Each of these perfumes have an olfactory luxuriousness and woody depth that can only be extracted from the purest Australian and global essences.

A true journey, each begins with the honest and elemental beauty of an Australian sunrise - fresh, crisp and pure, with aromatic and invigorating overtones. From this fresh, light, and joyous sunrise, journey to a floral, exotic, and simply dazzling fragrance summit where each perfume reveals its elixir of olfactory character.

NATIVE BOTANICAL CULTURE

Legend has it that Australian sandalwood trees are born and thrive on fields of goldâ€¦ The journey of each Goldfield & Banks perfume begins in Australia, where native botanicals are harvested and processed to create oils of exceptional purity. These are then combined locally with essences from around the globe, and are then sent to France to be blended with organic alcohol and bottled, resulting in the final perfumes which our clients know and love.

Each perfume is meticulously crafted by Dimitri Weber, the Founder and Creative Director of Goldfield & Banks, in collaboration with renowned perfumers from around the globe.For the selection of our native ingredients, we are fortunate to work with experienced and dedicated growers and suppliers of botanicals all over Australia who provide us with high-grade oils often unknown in the perfume industry today.

The sustainable quality of these ingredients represents an innovative and contemporary approach to perfume oil production, and showcases Australia's leading expertise in botanical research and development. The result is a modern yet timeless collection of genderless luxury perfumes for men and women.

ODE TO AUSTRALIA

Goldfield & Banks pays homage to Australia's first botanist and naturalist, Joseph Banks.

A curious explorer and prodigious collector, Banks' collection of Australian plants, seeds, insects, and wildlife was the first to reveal the vast botanical richness of this continent to the European world.

Likewise, we have gathered the finest ingredients from across the globe and combined them with the pure, natural essences drawn from Australia's diverse array of seeds, woods, flowers, lichens, resins, leaves and fruits.

THE FOUNDER

The Goldfield & Banks story actually begins on the other side of the world when the French-Belgian Dimitri Weber - like so many voyagers before him - set out on a journey to discover the curious, enigmatic land that lay far beyond the horizon.

Falling in love with Australia's lush, aromatic, and botanical beauty, its sweeping plains, turquoise-fringed coasts, ochre-red interiors and harsh, dramatic landscapes, this world-wandering fragrance expert created Goldfield & Banks Australia. In doing so, he captured the essences of Australia's stunning elements and made them his tribute to this unique, lustrous, and extraordinary continent.

In this quest to develop a botanical dream from a timeless land, Dimitri Weber created a luxurious and precious collection of gender-free fragrances featuring exceptional, pure, and rare Australian essences previously unexplored in modern perfumery.

OUR VALUES

To us, luxury is defined not as extravagance, but the use of the finest essences available without compromising the earth. It is for this reason that we only use essences sourced sustainably, even where the cost of this practice is significant (such as with Queensland Agarwood). Working with Australian growers and world-leading oil producers means that our perfumes leave the planet in the same or better condition than it was in before.

Examples of the high-grade native oils we use are Australian Sandalwood, Buddha Wood, Brown Boronia, Blue Cypress, and Golden Wattle. We also complement these with exotic ingredients that have been introduced to Australia's unique climate, such as Lavender and Agarwood.

Ethics are also an integral part of who we are, so all of our perfumes are 100% cruelty free, vegan, and meet the stringent standards of the International Fragrance Association (IFRA). All our perfumes are diluted in organic beetroot alcohol. We are one of the first brands to use this innovative ingredient in our collection.

TRACEABILITY

Since we began making perfumes in 2016, we have developed privileged partnerships with leaders in cultivating botanical species and essential oils.

We engage with only the most experienced growers, who are passionate about premium crops with full traceability from the field to the final product.

Their knowledge and accountability measures are unparalleled in this industry- we know exactly where each crop was grown, as well as more minute details such as the growing and harvesting conditions for each batch of oil.
